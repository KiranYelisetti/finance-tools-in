Applying for government jobs or competitive exams in India can be an exhausting process. One of the most frustrating roadblocks applicants face is uploading their photograph, signature, and certificate files on portal application forms.

Whether it is the UPSC, SSC, IBPS, or State PSC portals, each system enforces **strict rules regarding file format, pixel dimensions, and file size in Kilobytes (KB)**. If your file does not meet these requirements, the portal will reject it, or worse, your application could be disqualified.

## Dimension & Size Requirements for Top Indian Exams

Here are the standard photo and signature requirements for major recruitment and entrance exams in India:

### 1. UPSC (Union Public Service Commission)
* **Photograph:**
  * **Dimensions:** Minimum $350 \times 350$ pixels, Maximum $1000 \times 1000$ pixels (Width $\times$ Height).
  * **File Size:** **20 KB to 300 KB**.
  * **Important:** The photo must show the candidate's name and the date on which the photograph was taken printed at the bottom of the photo.
* **Signature:**
  * **Dimensions:** Minimum $350 \times 350$ pixels, Maximum $1000 \times 1000$ pixels.
  * **File Size:** **20 KB to 300 KB**.

### 2. SSC (Staff Selection Commission)
* **Photograph:**
  * **Dimensions:** $3.5\text{ cm width} \times 4.5\text{ cm height}$ (approx. $132 \times 170$ pixels).
  * **File Size:** **20 KB to 50 KB**.
  * **Important:** Photo must be taken without spectacles and cap, and both ears must be visible.
* **Signature:**
  * **Dimensions:** $4.0\text{ cm width} \times 2.0\text{ cm height}$ (approx. $151 \times 75$ pixels).
  * **File Size:** **10 KB to 20 KB** (Must be on white paper in black ink).

### 3. Banking Exams (IBPS / SBI / RBI)
* **Photograph:**
  * **Dimensions:** $4.5\text{ cm} \times 3.5\text{ cm}$ (or $200 \times 230$ pixels).
  * **File Size:** **20 KB to 50 KB**.
* **Signature:**
  * **Dimensions:** $140 \times 60$ pixels.
  * **File Size:** **10 KB to 20 KB** (Signature in CAPITAL letters is not accepted).
* **Left Thumb Impression:**
  * **Dimensions:** $240 \times 240$ pixels (or $3\text{ cm} \times 3\text{ cm}$).
  * **File Size:** **20 KB to 50 KB**.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid When Preparing Documents

1. **Incorrect File Format:** Most portals only accept **JPEG or JPG** formats. Trying to upload PNG, WebP, or PDF files for photos will cause errors.
2. **Signature in Blue Ink on Lined Paper:** Lenders and exam portals usually mandate black ink on white unruled paper. Avoid using ruled notebook paper, as lines interfere with optical character recognition.
3. **Blurred Images Due to Upscaling:** Taking a tiny 5 KB image and stretching it to meet a 50 KB requirement results in pixelation. Start with a high-quality capture and shrink it down instead.
4. **Incorrect Aspect Ratio:** Forcing a square photo into a rectangular slot makes the face appear stretched or squashed. Always crop the photo to the correct aspect ratio before resizing.
